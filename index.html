📋 Complete SREC-II Self-Management Guide

⚠️ CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS:
  • GATS Account Type: Select "GENERATOR" (NOT Broker or Aggregator)
  • Standing Order: Check the "automatically create standing order" box during registration
  • Monthly Deadline: Submit meter readings between 15th-20th of each month

✅ PHASE 1: GATS Account Creation

Step 1: Navigate to https://www.pjm-eis.com

  • Look for "GATS Sign In" section (top left corner)
  • Click "Not a Member? Register Here"

Step 2: Complete New Account Registration Form

  • Account Type: Select GENERATOR from dropdown (CRITICAL)
  • Organization Name: Your name or LLC
  • Contact Information: Email, phone, mailing address
  • Organization Type: Select "Individual" for residential

Step 3: Wait for Account Approval

  • Timeline: Typically approved next business day
  • Confirmation: Email from [email protected]
  • Action: Save your GATS username and password securely

✅ PHASE 2: Asset Registration (Solar Generator)

Understanding the "Standing Order":
The standing order is an automatic instruction that tells GATS: "Every time a certificate is created for my system, immediately transfer it to the Solar Incentives NJ Program account." Without this, your SRECs sit in YOUR account and InClime cannot see them or pay you. The standing order ensures: automatic transfer → automatic invoicing → automatic payment.

Step 4: Log Into Your GATS Account

  • URL: https://www.pjm-eis.com
  • Click "GATS Sign In" and enter credentials

Step 5: Navigate to Generator Registration

  • Click "Assets" tab in top navigation
  • Select "Register Solar Photovoltaic Generator" from dropdown

Step 6: Complete Generator Registration Form

  • Facility Name: Your address or custom name
  • State Certification Number: Enter NJ Certification Number EXACTLY as shown (format: NJ-XXXXXX-SUN-I-XX-SREC-II)
  • Nameplate Capacity: System size in kW (from As-Built Worksheet)
  • Commenced Operation Date: Date on PTO letter
  • Generation Entry Type: Choose "Meter Reading" (you'll enter lifetime kWh totals)
⚠️ CRITICAL STEP: Scroll to bottom of registration form and CHECK THE BOX: ☑️ "Check this box to automatically create standing order"

This checkbox is MANDATORY for automatic payment processing!

Step 7: Submit Generator Application

  • Click "Submit for Approval"
  • Timeline: 3-4 business days for approval
  • You'll Receive: GATS Unit ID (format: NON-XXXXXXX)

Step 8: Verify Standing Order Was Created

  • Log into GATS → Click "Certificates" tab → "View Standing Orders"
  • Verify: Recipient = "Solar Incentives NJ Program", Status = Active, Irrevocable = Yes

✅ PHASE 3: Payment Portal Registration

Step 9: Create Account on Solar Incentives NJ

  • URL: https://www.solarincentivesnj.com
  • Click "Create Account" (NOT "Login" yet)
  • Enter email and create password
  • Check email for activation link (including spam folder)

Step 10: Register Payment Account

  • Log in → Click "Projects" tab → "Register a Payment Account"

Step 11: Complete 5-Step Payment Application

  1. Step 1 - Property Location: Solar installation address, utility company
  2. Step 2 - System Owner: Owner name (must match NJCEP certification)
  3. Step 3 - Bank Account: Routing number, account number, account type
  4. Step 4 - System Registration: NJ Cert #, GATS Unit ID, system size, operation date
  5. Step 5 - Document Upload: Photo of voided check (REQUIRED - must match Step 3 info)

Step 12: Wait for Payment Account Approval

  • Timeline: 3-5 business days
  • Status Check: Log into SolarIncentivesNJ.com → Projects tab
  • "Approved" = Ready to receive payments

✅ PHASE 4: Monthly Meter Reading Submission

How Generation Converts to SREC-IIs:
• 1 SREC-II = 1 MWh (1,000 kWh) of production
• Your inverter tracks lifetime production (cumulative kWh)
• You report this lifetime number monthly
• GATS calculates monthly production automatically
• When cumulative production crosses 1,000 kWh threshold, GATS mints 1 SREC-II

Optimal Submission Window: 15th-20th of Each Month

  • Readings submitted by 20th → Certificate mints on last day of month → Included in next month's invoicing
  • Example: Feb 15-20 reading → Certificate mints Feb 28 → Utility invoiced in March → Payment in April

Step 13: Locate Your Lifetime Production Reading

  • Enphase: Open app → Lifetime energy production (kWh)
  • SolarEdge: Open monitoring portal → Total energy produced (kWh)
  • Record the LIFETIME CUMULATIVE number (not monthly)

Step 14: Enter Reading into GATS

  • Log into https://www.pjm-eis.com
  • Click "Assets" → Hover "Generation" → "Enter/View Generation"
  • Select your generator
  • Navigate to current month using arrows
  • In "Read" row: Enter lifetime production (e.g., 12450)
  • In "Date" box: Select reading date (must be 1st-15th)
  • Click "SAVE" → Click "OK"

Step 15: Certificate Minting (Automatic)

  • When: Last day of each month
  • Condition: Cumulative production reached next 1,000 kWh threshold
  • Transfer: Auto-transfers to "Solar Incentives NJ Program" (via standing order)
  • You don't need to do anything—this is automatic!

✅ PHASE 5: Payment Processing

Quarterly Payment Schedule:

  • Q1 (Jan-Mar production): Paid in April
  • Q2 (Apr-Jun production): Paid in July
  • Q3 (Jul-Sep production): Paid in October
  • Q4 (Oct-Dec production): Paid in January of following year

Payment Amount: Number of MWh produced × $85

  • Example: 3 MWh in Q1 = 3 × $85 = $255 paid in April

Monitor Payment Status:

  • Log into https://www.solarincentivesnj.com → Projects tab
  • Payments deposited to bank account registered in Phase 3

Understanding Carryover Math:
  • 1 SREC-II = 1,000 kWh (1 MWh) - Certificates only mint in whole units
  • Example Month 1: Produce 800 kWh → 0 MWh minted → 800 kWh carries over
  • Example Month 2: Produce 750 kWh → Cumulative: 800 + 750 = 1,550 kWh → 1 MWh minted ($85 earned) → 550 kWh carries over
  • Example Month 3: Produce 850 kWh → Cumulative: 550 + 850 = 1,400 kWh → 1 MWh minted ($85 earned) → 400 kWh carries over
